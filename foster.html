Fostering Expectations!

Please adhere to the following expectations to foster an animal

  1. Foster parents must be 18 years or older, or have parental consent.
  2. Any current pets in the foster parent's home must be up to date in county tags and vaccinations.
  3. If your property is rented, it is your responsibility to ensure that you are allowed to foster.
  4. Foster parents should provide a safe environment for their foster pet where they can receive socialization, exercise, and plenty of love!
